50 years of IHA!

For five decades, the International Huntington Association has been supporting the global HD community. We have been raising awareness, promoting drug trials, lobbying for services and connecting people and organisations from all around the world. As we celebrate this remarkable milestone, we stand determined in our commitment to make a difference. Our 50th-anniversary celebration marks not only a milestone in our history but also the beginning of important new initiatives, projects aimed at furthering our impact, reaching ever more people and bringing hope and comfort to all.
